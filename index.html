Let's explore the life of Kanye West

Kanye Omari West is a 44-year-old American rapper, record producer, and fashion designer. He was born in Atlanta and raised in Chicago. Ye is today one of the most influential person in the world with millions of followers all over the world. He is also considered as one of the wealthiest artists in the world with a net worth of 1,8 billion dollars. He released a total of 14 albums with millions of copies sold. From Louis Vuitton to Nike he has done many collaborations but he decided to create it's own brand called YEEZY. It is one of the most popular clothing/sneaker brand in the fashion industry today. Often criticized and misunderstood this multi-talented man still has many surprises in store for us and he will keep on growing on what he has already built
